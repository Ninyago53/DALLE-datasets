Die California Clipper in der Bucht von Manila, ca. 1940
The Boeing 314 California Clipper (civil registration NC18602) off the Cavite Navy Yard, Philippines, 1939-1941. Delivered on 27 January 1939, it went to the USAAF as C-98 42-88632, 18 December 1941; then to the US Navy as BuNo 99084. It was sold to Universal Airways in 1946, and to American International in 1948. It was finally scrapped in 1950.
